Chocolate Cake

115g / 4oz / 2/3cup raw cane sugar
4tsp cocoa or carob powder
1/2tsp sea salt
170g / 6oz / 1 1/2cups wholewheat flour
3/4 tsp bicarbonate of soda (baking soda)
90ml / 3floz / 1/3cup vegetable oil
1tsp vanilla essence
2tsp cider vinegar or wine vinegar
200ml / 1/3pt / 3/4 cup cold water

1) Mix thoroughly the sugar, cocoa (or carob), salt, flour and soda in a mixing bowl.
2) Add the oil, vanilla essence and vinegar and pour cold water over the mixture.
3) Combine well with a fork, but do not beat.
4) Pour into two greased sandwich tins (lined if desired) and bake in a moderate oven at 350F/180C/Gas Mark 4 for 30 minutes or until the cake springs back when lightly pressed. Leave to cool thoroughly.

Chocolate Icing

55g / 2oz / 1/4cups vegan margarine
30g / 1oz / 1/4cup cocoa or carob powder
4 tbs soya (soy) milk
140g / 5oz / 5/6cup raw cane sugar
1tsp vanilla essence

1) Combine the first four ingredients in a saucepan and bring to the boil slowly, stirring constantly.
2) Boil for 1 minute.
3) Remove from the heat and beat until cold. Add the vanilla essence and spread the icing on the cake.
